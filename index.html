I run the questions your customers are already asking — about your business, your trade, your area — live, in front of you, across the AI systems people actually use. You see exactly what's said, whether you're named at all, and who's being recommended instead. No slides, no theory — your real business, live on screen.

Do I need to understand AI to work with you?

No. Most people I work with have never opened ChatGPT. That's fine — this isn't about learning a tool, it's about understanding what a tool already says about you. Everything's explained in plain English, and you walk away knowing exactly where you stand, not with homework.

How is this different from SEO?

SEO gets you ranked in a list of links. This is about something newer: when someone asks an AI system a question directly and it gives one answer, not ten — is your business the one it names? That's a different, growing part of how customers find businesses now, and most SEO work doesn't touch it at all.

Do you only work with Brisbane businesses?

I'm based in Brisbane and that's where my own proof of work comes from, but the service itself runs entirely live over video — so I work with businesses across Australia. Where you're based doesn't change what AI systems say about you, or how it gets fixed.

What if the audit finds nothing wrong?

Then you'll know that for certain, instead of guessing — and that's genuinely useful too. I'm not going to manufacture a problem to sell you a fix. If your business is already well represented, I'll tell you so.

Some things move within weeks. Others — particularly showing up when someone asks a general question without naming your business — take longer and depend on building genuine, ongoing presence, not a single fix. I'll always tell you honestly which is which, rather than promising a timeline I can't back up.
